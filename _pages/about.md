I am a Ph.D. student in the computer science program at Iowa State University (ISU). Currently, I work in the laboratory for software design with my supervisor, [Dr. Hridesh Rajan](https://www.cs.iastate.edu/hridesh). Before I decided to move to ISU, I was enrolled at UND and took some graduate-level courses. Prior to that, I received my undergraduate degree in Software Engineering from Addis Ababa Science and Technology University.

Research
------
My research interests broadly lays at the intersection of software engineering, data science, and deep learning. At present, I work on a shared data science infrastructure for bioinformatics,[Boag](http://boa.cs.iastate.edu/boag/index.php).
