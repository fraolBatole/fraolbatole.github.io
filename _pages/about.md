I am a Ph.D. student in the computer science program at Iowa State University (ISU). Currently, I work in the laboratory for software design with my supervisor, [Dr. Hridesh Rajan](https://www.cs.iastate.edu/hridesh). Before I decided to move to ISU, I was enrolled at UND and took some graduate-level courses for a year. Prior to that, I received my undergraduate degree in Software Engineering from Addis Ababa Science and Technology University with a great distinction.

Research
------
My research interests broadly lays at the intersection of software engineering, data science, and deep learning. Specifically, my research focuses on the use of software engineering methodologies to enhance deep learning models. Prior to that, I worked on a shared data science infrastructure for bioinformatics,[Boag](http://boa.cs.iastate.edu/boag/index.php).
